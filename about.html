About Us

Wild Rescue is dedicated to protecting and rehabilitating injured, orphaned, and displaced wildlife. Our goal is to rescue animals in need, provide safe rehabilitation, and release them back into their natural habitat.

Our Mission

  • Rescue injured and orphaned wildlife
  • Provide rehabilitation and medical support
  • Release animals safely back into the wild
  • Educate the community on wildlife safety
